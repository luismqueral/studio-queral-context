Genghis Khan conquered the largest contiguous land empire in history through unmatched speed, adaptability, and communication.

* What would ruthless mobility and lightweight operations allow you to accomplish that your more settled competitors cannot?
* How could you create a network where information travels faster than in surrounding systems?
* What traditions or attachments might you need to abandon to achieve maximum flexibility?

Consider the Mongol approach: travel light, incorporate useful elements from conquered territories, maintain discipline across vast distances, and use speed as your primary advantage. How might you become the force that others must adapt to, rather than the other way around?
