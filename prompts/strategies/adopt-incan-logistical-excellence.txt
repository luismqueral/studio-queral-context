The Inca Empire governed a vast territory across challenging Andean terrain through remarkable organization, record-keeping, and infrastructure.

* How might you develop communication and transportation systems that overcome geographical or organizational barriers?
* What form might your khipu (knotted cord records) take to track complex information without conventional writing?
* How could you implement systems of reciprocal obligation to ensure contribution and loyalty?

Consider the Incan approach: build highways connecting distant regions, maintain storehouses to manage surplus and scarcity, use standardized architectural templates, and implement mita labor taxation for public works. What mountains would your royal road traverse?
