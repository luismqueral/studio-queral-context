The explosion of artistic and intellectual achievement during the Renaissance was fueled by strategic patronage from wealthy families, popes, and princes.

*   What nascent talent or promising ideas in your environment need protection and resources to flourish?
*   How might you act as a Medici or Borgia, cultivating brilliance while also advancing your strategic interests?
*   What balance of freedom and direction creates the most productive creative environment?

Consider how concentrated investment in exceptional talent—giving them both challenge and shelter—might yield innovations that elevate your entire enterprise. Who are your potential Michelangelos, and what is your Sistine Chapel?
