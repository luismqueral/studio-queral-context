The Phoenicians built a maritime commercial network across the Mediterranean through superior navigation, strategic outposts, and cultural-linguistic adaptability.

* What specialized expertise could make you an invaluable intermediary?
* How might establishing a chain of outposts extend your reach beyond competitors?
* What alphabet (simplified communication system) could you develop to facilitate exchange?

Consider the Phoenician approach: develop specialized transportation capability, create colonies that serve as trade hubs, maintain cultural flexibility while preserving core identity, and find valuable materials to transform and redistribute. What would be your purple dye or cedar wood?
