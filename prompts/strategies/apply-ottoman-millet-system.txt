The Ottoman Empire governed a remarkably diverse population by granting significant autonomy to different religious and ethnic communities while maintaining central authority.

* How might creating semi-autonomous units with clear responsibilities and boundaries improve function?
* What overarching frameworks are necessary to maintain cohesion while allowing diversity?
* How could you systematically incorporate foreign talent and expertise?

Consider the Ottoman approach: allow communities to govern their internal affairs according to their own traditions, collect defined contributions while providing protection, and create advancement paths for outsiders with valuable skills. How might you become a Sublime Porte for diverse stakeholders?
