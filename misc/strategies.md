# Strategy Cards

These are creative thinking prompts designed to help you approach design challenges from unexpected angles. Each strategy offers a different lens through which to view your project.

### How do I use these?

Pick a strategy that feels relevant (or deliberately irrelevant) to your current challenge. Here's a sample prompt you might use:

```
I'm working on [describe your project briefly]. 

Please apply the "Honor the Shadows" strategy to help me think about this differently. 

Use the NYT design system assets from this repository as needed.
```

### Random Strategy Selection

For broader exploration, try this approach in Cursor's "ask" mode:

```
Select 5 random strategies from the list below and apply each one to [describe your project] and give me distinct perspectives on how to approach this challenge.

Use the NYT design system assets from this repository as needed.
```

## Honor the Shadows

*Theme: Psychology*

```
What exists in the shadows of this project, unacknowledged?

Identify the overlooked aspects, the negative spaces, or the deliberately ignored elements. Explore in detail how bringing these shadows into focus might transform your understanding or approach. Sometimes the most profound insights are found not in the light, but in what we've chosen to leave in the dark.
```
